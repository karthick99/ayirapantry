Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between cold pressed and refined oil?

Cold pressed oils are extracted at low temperatures retaining all natural nutrients and flavor, whereas refined oils are chemically treated and heated, stripping away nutrients.

Do you add any preservatives?

No. Ayira Pantry oils are 100% natural with zero preservatives, additives, or chemicals. Just the pure oil.

How long does delivery take?

We usually dispatch within 24 hours. Delivery takes 2-4 days for South India and 5-7 days for rest of India.

Is Cash on Delivery available?

Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) across most pin codes in India.
