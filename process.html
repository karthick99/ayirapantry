How It's Made

Transparency from Farm to Bottle.

1

Sourcing the Best

We don't buy oils; we make them. It starts with selecting the highest quality seeds directly from farmers who use traditional farming methods.

2

Sun Drying

Seeds are sun-dried to reduce moisture content naturally. This prevents mold and ensures the oil has a rich, nutty flavor.

3

Wood Pressing (Mara Sekku)

The seeds are crushed in a traditional wood chekku at very low speeds. This prevents heat generation ( Cold Pressed), keeping vital nutrients intact.

4

Natural Settling

We don't filter our oils using machines or chemicals. The oil is allowed to settle naturally for 3-5 days in sterile stainless steel tanks.

5

Hygienic Packing

The clear supernatant oil is then carefully bottled and sealed, ready to be delivered to your doorstep.

Bringing traditional purity back to your kitchen with temperature-controlled cold pressed oils.
